Goals

The goals for the vollyball website include, advertising the local vollyball team to their community. This is an effort to increase their customers by 50%, including families, which is a good amount of players for a single team. The site needs to inform the community of their licenced bar, available to hire functions and fitness. The most important goal for the site is to attract a younger audience. which, in return, will lead to more young people coming to play with their friends, teams and weekend competitions resulting in more people paying to come and attend the games.

Success Evaluation

The success of the site will be measured by the amount of people that have clicked the links displayed on the site. Also the amount of people that have used the contact information displayed on the website. Most of all the success of the website will be meausred by the amount of people that have registered their teams to play vollyball by filling out the registry form. Primarily the success of the website will be based on the amount of people who attend the games. If more people attend than ususal than the site will be ruled as successful.

Target Audience

The target audience for this site is 15 years to 30 years of age. The site needs to attract younger people in high school in order to also attract their friends and build teams. This will result in the younger peoples parents showing up to watch their kids play against other teams. The parents, of course, epresenting the older ages closer to 30.
